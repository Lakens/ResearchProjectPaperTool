Participants
Participants were 439 college students enrolled at a midsize Midwestern university in the United States.
The sample was 71.9% female and 28.1% male; and predominantly (81.7%) white; predominantly able-bodied (95.2%); and predominantly heterosexual (91.5%).
The average age was 22.97 years (SD = 6.62).
Eighty-seven percent of the students were enrolled in undergraduate programs, 11.4% were in graduate programs, and .7% were non-degreeseeking students.
