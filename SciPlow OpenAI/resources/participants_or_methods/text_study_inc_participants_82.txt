Participants
Fifty-three community-dwelling individuals diagnosed with either schizophrenia or schizoaffective disorder according to the Structured Clinical Interview for the DSM-IV (First et al., 1995) and stabilized on antipsychotic medication participated.
They were treated in accordance with institutional review procedures and written informed consent was obtained in all cases.
Exclusion criteria included auditory and/or visual impairment, mental retardation (MR) as evidenced by a history of MR services, lack of fluency in the English language, history of a traumatic brain injury and/or a neurologic illness, or active substance abuse or dependence.
Forty-nine participants were recruited from the Schizophrenia Rehabilitation Program (SRP), an intensive outpatient setting at the Institute of Living in Hartford, CT. and 4 participants were recruited from Intercommunity Mental Health Center, a community clinic in East Hartford, CT.
